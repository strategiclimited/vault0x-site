What is
Vault0x

Vault0x is a Wearable Hardware Wallet for Cryptocurrencies and other digital assets to keep them highly safe & secure while accessing them on the go.

Vault0x gives you the flexibility to use the device as wallet, watch or 2FA device without worrying about losing it or misplacing it.

What are
Hardware Wallets

Hardware wallets are the safest way to store cryptocurrencies without getting hacked or phished.

Hardware wallet can generate new accounts while offline and never expose their private key to outside world. They cannot be monitored or controlled by any exchange or government. With hardware wallet you are the only one with access to your cryptos. They can also be used for storing other digital assets and carrying of 2FA authentication.
